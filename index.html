How to Build a Strategy
SPES strategies are built from three layers: Indicators (what to measure), Conditions (when to act), and a Risk Profile (how much to risk).
1
Add Indicators
Select up to 5 indicators. These are the data sources your conditions reference — Fast MA, Slow MA, RSI, MACD, ATR, etc.
2
Long / Short Conditions
Each condition: A [operator] B. Example: Fast MA crosses above Slow MA. ALL conditions must be true at the same bar.
3
Exit Rules
Fixed SL/TP, ATR-based (dynamic volatility-adjusted), trailing stop, or opposite signal.
4
Filters
Optional extra gates — "only trade when ATR is above X" or "only trade when RSI is not extreme".
5
Risk Profile
Select a named profile. Controls capital, position sizing, max drawdown, pyramiding. Shared across strategies.

Pyramiding — Scale Into Winners
Add to a winning position in tranches. Each addition is funded by floating profit. Stop loss moves up to keep total risk flat — you're letting the market pay for the next entry.
Entry Plan Preview
ELF Pyramid Strategy
Pyramid triggered by R-multiple gains. SL = Donchian band locked at entry (lower for longs, upper for shorts). After each add, SL shifts to keep total open risk constant at the strategy's risk%. TP = reverse entry signal. Re-entry requires a fresh pullback setup after each close.

Slippage Model
Simulates realistic fill deviation at entry, SL hit, and TP hit.
Uses ATR to scale slippage dynamically with volatility.
Applied At
▲ Long EntryFill price = open + slippage
▼ Short EntryFill price = open − slippage
▲ Long SL HitFill price = SL − slippage
▼ Short SL HitFill price = SL + slippage
▲ Long TP HitFill price = TP − slippage
▼ Short TP HitFill price = TP + slippage
Slippage model is OFF. The backtest assumes fills exactly at next-bar open, SL, and TP prices.
Enable to simulate realistic market friction — especially important for volatile assets and lower timeframes.

Funding Rate Model
Simulates futures funding charges applied every 8 hours.
Scales automatically with your chart timeframe and bars held.
Typical range: 0.005% – 0.03% for major pairs in normal conditions.
Can spike to 0.1%+ during extreme volatility or high leverage demand.
How It's Applied
FrequencyEvery 8h — scaled to chart TF
▲ Long positionsPay funding each period
▼ Short positionsReceive funding each period
Charge = position_size × price × rate%
Funding rate model is OFF. Long-held positions incur no carry cost in the backtest.
Enable to simulate realistic futures funding — especially important for trades held multiple days.
